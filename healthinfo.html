Nutrition

Eat a balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins. Limit processed foods, sugar, and unhealthy fats to maintain overall health.

Exercise

Aim for at least 30 minutes of moderate exercise most days of the week. Activities like walking, cycling, or swimming boost physical and mental well-being.

Common Health Issues

Stay informed about common health concerns like diabetes, heart disease, and obesity. Regular check-ups, healthy habits, and early detection can help prevent or manage these conditions effectively.
